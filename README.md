🔹 Stayopx – AI-Powered Hospitality Intelligence Dashboard

Stayopx is an AI-driven analytics and intelligence platform for the hospitality industry. Designed for hotels, resorts, and property managers, it provides actionable insights across operations, costs, assets, and guest experience — all in a centralised, intuitive dashboard.

🔹 🚀 Key Features & Modules
Organogram Management – Visualise and optimise organisational structure and staff efficiency.
Property Analytics – Track occupancy, revenue performance, and property utilisation.
Food & Menu Analytics – Analyse menu performance, food costs, demand trends, and profitability.
Cost & Expense Analytics – Monitor operational costs, utility usage, and overhead expenses.
Guest Experience Analytics – Improve customer satisfaction, service quality, and experience trends.
Asset Management – Analyse rental vs. purchased assets, CAPEX, OPEX, and overall asset performance.
Operations Insights – Get actionable recommendations for staffing, resource allocation, and facility optimisation.
🔹 📊 Advanced Intelligence & Analytics
AI-Powered Insights – Predict demand, optimise pricing, and identify operational inefficiencies.
Financial & Cost Optimisation – Track OPEX, CAPEX, utilities, and other operational costs.
Comprehensive Dashboard – Centralised view of property, food, menu, cost, guest experience, and asset analytics.
🔹 💻 Technology Stack
Frontend: HTML, CSS, JavaScript
Libraries & Tools: Data visualisation and analytics libraries for interactive dashboards
Analytics & Reporting: AI-driven insights integrated into dynamic charts, graphs, and KPIs
